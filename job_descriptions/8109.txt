The Data Assistant provides solutions and analysis for the Sales Operations Builder Multifamily channel by performing a variety of tasks and ad hoc projects as required by management.

Data Assistants evaluate internal file systems for efficiency, problems, and inaccuracies, developing and maintaining protocols for handling, processing, and cleaning data. The ideal candidate should be highly skilled in all aspects of data analytics. Additionally, you should be committed to transforming data into readable, goal-driven reports for continued innovation and growth. This role will work with builder and multifamily industry sales data.

Locate and validate contract information in SAP and Siebel
Review of historical portfolio data and upload entries into SAP
