Responsibilities
Senior Analytics Consultant role will be part of a data analytics team supporting a large Intelligence Community Agency with data process improvement, data management, and creating performance metrics and business intelligence for business optimization. The candidate will be required to analyze large and complex data sets, including a thorough aptitude for conducting quantitative and qualitative analyses; selecting and applying the appropriate analytical techniques in order to increase organizational efficiency and provide knowledge of data engineering/analysis applications to extract knowledge to help predict, explain, or analyze research questions. The candidate will coordinate with non-technical users to identify data analytics needs support and use facilitation skills and techniques to elicit requirements from a diverse range of stakeholders.
The candidate should be comfortable in working with cross-functional teams to present, analyze, update, and view technical data sets with qualitative and statistical analysis. The candidate must have the ability to translate complex problem sets, metrics and assessments into coherent, communications products to brief senior-level officials.
Assess data and analytics capabilities and identify areas for improvement or opportunities to better align data operations with the business or mission strategy
Implement data management practices and frameworks
 Collaborate with the Government to develop, integrate and continually improve data, including the creation and visualization of metrics
Work independently and as a team member on assigned data and/or process improvement projects
 Create data visualizations based on customer requirements using business intelligence tools such as Tableau
 Conduct iterative customer feedback sessions on dashboards in development
 Learn and apply change management techniques to customers to assist them with adopting new data analytics tools and associated processes
