Job Description:
Our great client in Milwaukee, WI has an immediate remote opening for a Data Science Analyst. If you are interested please apply with resume to aboettcher@apexsystems.com.

As a Data Science Analyst, you will work in close collaboration with the Data Science team to understand Data Science products, assists in the development and/or modification of new and existing products and functionality to adapt to evolving business needs (e.g., envisioning end-user experience, identifying needs of business-end consumers). Additional responsibilities include:

Submit routine and ad-hoc queries to existing Data Science products in response to business team requests (e.g., leverage existing forecasting model(s) to produce weekly forecast updates on key business metrics).
Utilize Data Science product outputs to develop routine and ad-hoc reports in response to requests from the business (e.g., read-in, filter, merge and/or summarize various data tables; compose narrative summaries of analytic findings).
Facilitate delivery of new reporting solutions. Work with business owners to assess requests and define parameters.
Manage and prioritize competing interests. Identify and conduct needs and value assessments for reporting requests from the business while maintaining and optimizing existing reports.
Proactively conduct reviews of existing reports for accuracy, integrity and relevance to the business.
Responsible for unique and ongoing responsibilities as assigned.
