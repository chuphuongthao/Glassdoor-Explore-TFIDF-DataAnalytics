Ph.D. Scientist - R&D
We are seeking a highly motivated PhD Scientists to join the R&D organization of the Procter and Gamble Co, located in Cincinnati, OH. R&D Scientists are responsible for inventing, reapplying, developing, and delivering breakthrough product, process, raw material and packaging technologies that provide irresistible and winning brand experience to consumers. They work on cutting-edge technologies with multi-disciplinary and highly professional teams across the Company. In R&D, we have opportunities in a variety of technical fields like: Product Research, Process Development, Analytical, Modeling and Simulation, Product Safety and Regulatory Affairs. The Scientist will work in close collaboration with multifunctional project teams and lead upstream innovation programs to evaluate new molecules, understand fundamental mechanisms, generate new insights, and develop R&D solutions that deliver superior benefits in our consumer products, such as Tide, Dawn, Pantene, Olay, Pampers, Bounty, Always, Crest, Nyquil, Gillette, and many more, to further grow P&G's $75+ billion business. These insights will have a meaningful influence on our business strategy and will enable our products to win with consumers and deliver sustainable results for our Company.
Qualifications

All interested candidates must be seeking / possess a PhD degree in a STEM field such as Chemistry (Organic, Inorganic, Bio, Analytical, Physical), Engineering (Chemical, Mechanical, Material Science, Biomedical), Life Sciences (Biology, Microbiology, Bioinformatics, toxicology, pharmacology), or a related field.
Candidates must possess excellent problem-solving skills, critical thinking and ability to use data to connect multiple ideas and develop solutions
Passionate about hands-on experimental work and demonstrated track record of leading, building and testing technical hypothesis
Strong ownership & self-leadership skills to understand the technical problem to be solved, collaborate with the project team for test method development and technology development
Collaborative team player with the ability to work in a multi-functional organization
Excellent oral and written communication skills in English
Publications track record in peer-reviewed scientific journals
A basic understanding of data science/statistics
Desired Qualifications:
Ability to code in main languages (Python, R, Matlab)
Experience in computational modeling and data analysis
Experience in more than one STEM field
