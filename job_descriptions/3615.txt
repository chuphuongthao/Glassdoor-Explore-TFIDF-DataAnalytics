Job summary
The Return to Work program at Amazon is a 16- week paid returnship for experienced professionals returning to the workforce after taking time off. The program is open to candidates who have at least 2 years of professional experience and have been out of the paid workforce for at least 6 months caregiving for kids or loved ones, serving in the military, or time off to find their path. If you meet these criteria, we welcome you to apply.

The Return to Work program inspires technologists to relaunch after taking a break in their career. This platform gives candidates an opportunity to work at Amazon, and provides on-the-job training to sharpen their professional skills while working on real world customer solutions. Participants will get training on SDE tools and technologies and technical mentoring.

Come join us as Data Engineer Intern building robust, intuitive, feature rich data solutions for our valued customers.

Responsibilities -


Understand our data and leverage the warehouse to build solutions for our internal users.

Leverage SQL and build pipelines and transformations/business logic to provide the data for our internal customers.

Production support involved with monitoring our Redshift clusters and pipelines and remediating issues

Participate and lead peer code reviews

Upon successful completion of the Returnship Program, candidates may receive, but are not guaranteed, an offer for full-time employment.Basic Qualifications

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Finance or related technical field.
1+ years of industry experience in software development, data engineering, business intelligence, data science, or related field with a track record of manipulating, processing, and extracting value from large datasets
Some Experience with data modeling, data warehousing, and building ETL pipelines
Some Experience and moderate knowledge of SQL
Preferred Qualifications

Is not currently enrolled in a Bachelors or Masters degree program

2+ years experience

Self starter with strong work ethic with a passion for problem-solving
Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic environment
Flexibility and ability to adapt to changing priorities and technologies
