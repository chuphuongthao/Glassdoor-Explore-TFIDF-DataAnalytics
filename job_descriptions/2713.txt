About this role:

Wells Fargo is seeking a Senior Strategy and Planning Consultant within the Artificial Intelligence / Machine Learning Center of Excellence (AI/ML CoE). The AI/ML CoE is a centralize team within Data Management and Insights (DMI) and provides model development and AI platform services across Wells Fargo. This role will report to the head AI Strategy and Governance, which is part of the Enterprise Analytics and Data Science (EADS) organization. The AI/ML CoE has ~200 employees (Data Scientists, Data Engineers and Analytic SMEs) across US and India.


In this role, you will:
Lead or participate in developing and implementing high impact and transformative strategic initiatives related to Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Natural Language Processes, Cloud, Model Development practices and processes, Analytics and model governance
Assist in the identification of strategic business, investment opportunities, evaluation, and approval process of identified opportunities
Review and analyze business, operational, tools and/or technical processes that require research, evaluation, and selection of alternatives. Develop position papers and recommendations
Identify opportunities and take initiative to resolve moderately complex issues
Lead team to meet deliverables while leveraging solid understanding of the function, policies, procedures, governance and compliance requirements
Collaborate and partner with Analytic leaders, Data Scientists and Analytic SMEs across EADS, AI/ML CoE, Technology, Model Governance. Partners based in US and India
Deliver strategic plans, positions and recommendations
Become an expert in latest AI/ML technologies and trends
Lead cross-functional teams to develop and implement key initiatives
Be a strategic thinker, problem solver and change agent
Take initiative to identify new opportunities
Use metrics to tell the story and value of AI
Lead funding and planning exercises
Build business cases to tell the story and value of AI
Create executive decks and materials
Manage and run routines with key stakeholders as needed

Contribute to large scale planning related to Strategy & Planning
