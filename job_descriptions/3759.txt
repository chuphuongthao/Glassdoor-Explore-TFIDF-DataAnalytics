Opportunity

The Data Science Analyst is obsessed with breaking down large data sets to identify trends, opportunities that drive decision making and deliver real business impact to MrBeast Burger. This individual is excited to look at the relationship between strategies, tactics, operations and overall performance. Highly skilled in all aspects of data analytics, the Analyst will identify trends and create models, transforming data into readable, goal-driven reports for continued innovation and growth. To thrive in this role, you will tinker with data regularly and have a proven track record of adding value through business analysis, producing analytics and interpreting results to deliver data-driven insight & recommendations.

WHAT YOU'LL DO
Serve as a trusted partner to the leadership teams across MrBeast and MrBeast Burger
Find innovative ways to grow the business, solve problems and inform strategies through data analysis and insights
Obsess over testing frameworks with our marketing team to improve performance with each campaign
Carry out analysis work, interpret results and make recommendations to ensure continuous improvement of operational activities across the business
Develop and maintain queries and categories to mine for meaningful data
Carry out analysis work, interpret and make recommendations to ensure continuous improvement for all projects, applying statistical techniques where appropriate
Develop, implement, and maintain leading-edge analytic systems, taking complicated problems and building simple frameworks
Proactively analyze data to answer key questions from stakeholders or out of self-initiated curiosity with an eye for what drives business performance, investigating and communicating areas for improvement in efficiency, productivity, processes and procedures
Ensure that data outputs are delivered on-time, within scope and within target expectations
Write best practice reports and presentations to summarize analysis, results and recommendations; deliver these to project managers and senior stakeholders based on data mining, analysis and visualization
Attend all training required in support of your role and as necessary to develop additional skills and competencies

WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR

An insanely curious individual who loves to solve complex problems
3+ years experience working in an analytical role
Extensive experience of data manipulation and visualization in Excel including charts, complex formulas and pivot tables
Experience with SQL databases including design, maintenance and application of tables and queries
The ability to explain basic statistical terms to non-technical stakeholders
Mapping out processes and making recommendations for improvement, for example, using Lean techniques
Ability to present in a clear and concise manner to various levels across the business, both verbally and in written documentation
Strong data evaluation skills
Master's in Business Administration or Finance preferred.
