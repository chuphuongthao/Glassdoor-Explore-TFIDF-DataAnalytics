Job Duties and Responsibilities:

The Data Analytics Engineer is responsible for building the DISH Wireless Commercial data integrations and implementing the fully-automated analytics modules catering to DISH business team needs and the analytics product offerings in the marketplace for Commercial customers. The engineer will focus on building the data pipelines, insights, KPIs and metrics using the DISH Wireless data from BSS, OSS, NSS or external sources to business/partners/customers. This individual will play an active role in delivery and ensure alignment to the end-state architecture.
Day-to-day job responsibilities:
Provide technical expertise in Wireless Analytics
Understand business needs and analytics product offerings to design and build data and analytics solutions
Analyze business and technical requirements
Quickly build prototypes to test out proof-of-concepts
Address technical concerns, ideas and suggestions
Assist in solving technical problems when they arise
Monitor systems to ensure they meet the partner, user and business goals
Deliver solutions, features using agile delivery methodologies
