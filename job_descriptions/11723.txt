OVERVIEW:
Senior Engineer, Data Analytics, Performance Assurance will drive insights from the 5G/4G EUD (end user device) ecosystem – IoT, Devices/Laptops/Tablets (Android and iOS), scanners, AGV, AMR, Video cameras and MEC, Cloud per industry vertical and use case. SME will be also responsible for conducting deep 5G/4G technology data insights from network, industry vertical, use cases and customer/business insights perspective to help enable data-based decisions. We’re looking for a Data Analytics SME to drive key 4G/5G technology and business insights for e2e performance assurance across different core, ran, security vendors, EUD ecosystem, MEC, Cloud etc. per industry vertical and use case to help deliver best in class end user experience.
ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
Develop centralized visualization, analytics platform for 5G/4G EUD ecosystem – Devices/Laptops/Tablets (Android and iOS), scanners, AGV, AMR, Video cameras
Develop centralized visualization, analytics solution for IoT, utilizing top manufacturing protocols (e.g., Ethernet/IP, Ether Cat, Ethernet Powerlink, Serco’s, Modbus TCP, ProfNet RT/IRT, MQTT)
Evaluate and shortlist potential IoT solutions for offer (e.g., Telit’s Device Wise and Qualcomm).
Drive deep analysis of e2e data 5G/4G nodes to enable data-based decisions based on the key performance indicators from 5G/4G – RAN / O-RAN RIC, nRIC, Core, Device, SAS
Explore new areas to drive insights from cloud - Google / Azure analytics to drive customer experience, business insights and efficiency metrics
Explore new areas to drive insights from MEC analytics to drive customer experience, business insights and efficiency metrics
Develop data ingestion, processing and analytics for the complete RAN, CORE, EUD ecosystem to get real understanding of customer experience for different use cases
Drive one stop shop performance assurance platform development with a modular approach
Design and develop automated interactive dashboards for reporting and data visualization for performance assurance and business metrics and insights
5G/4G centralized analytics, anomaly detection and automatic RCA using AI/ML
Drive to deliver a unified NMS / Active Performance Assurance solution for multivendor CORE, RAN, EUD ecosystem
Combine knowledge of several mathematical and statistical domains to help proactive analytics and ultimately deliver predictive analytics
Deliver performance and benchmarking insights from in-lab automation for load, dynamic radio/technology, handover
Deliver performance and benchmarking insights from in-field automation
Prepare, extract, manipulate and transform structured and unstructured data from many disparate data sources using Python, SQL, and other programmatic tools
Build predictive models and machine-learning algorithms to enable anomaly detection
Exposure to multiple SQL and NOSQL databases
