Department Overview
PG&E’s Transportation Services team is responsible for operating and managing one of the largest commercial fleets in the U.S. PG&E has pioneered efficient and sustainable green transportation technologies as part of our commitment to provide safe, reliable and affordable gas and electric service. PG&E’s industry leading green fleet includes a range of electric vehicle technologies—from hybrid electric bucket trucks to extended range pickups. Transportation Services is nationally recognized as a leader in green fleet technology and continues to innovate, invest and work closely with our industry, regulators, automakers and other partners to develop policies and best practices.

The Transportation Services department is responsible for managing all aspects of PG&E’s vehicles & equipment fleet, from design to disposition. The team is comprised multiple operating departments including Engineering, Field Operations, Business Operations, Strategy and Analytics. Operating departments within Transportation Services are responsible for company-wide management of over 14,000 vehicles & equipment, regulatory and compliance requirements, capital planning, clean air and alternate fuel initiatives, project support, company policy review, safety and technical analyses.

Work location is flexible within PG&E territory; final location will be determined by the hiring leader and successful candidate. You must be able to attend meetings in the San Ramon location as needed.

Position Summary
The Business Analysis and Applications team is a high-profile group that provides a unique environment for talented professionals who are passionate, dedicated & enthusiastic about participating in high-visibility projects, engaging in the heart of the business, and have built their career on making ongoing meaningful contributions that drive the success of the organization.

The team is responsible for providing comprehensive hands-on operational and analytical support for PG&E’s Transportation Services organization. The team also focuses on a variety of cross-functional value-added services including; project management & execution, in-depth financial & operational analysis/analytics, operational reporting & development, performance reporting, vehicle diagnostics, vehicle maintenance and part analytics and modeling, long-term strategic planning & strategic initiative execution, as well as support and enhancement of Transportation’s suite of information systems and applications that keep the fleet running.
