Job Duties and Responsibilities:

The Transport Specialist will manage all activities related to implementation of transport circuits within a given market including but not limited to the following:
Develop analytic methodologies to identify implementation related issues, recommend and lead implementation on areas of opportunity within key initiatives
Review and analyze Telco ready requirements and ensuring that they are properly defined for construction team
Lead periodic meetings with transport vendor(s) to ensure that circuits are delivered on time
Work with market deployment team and transport vendor to conduct site visits
Work with market deployment team to ensure that NTP is issued to the transport vendor promptly
Attend deployment meeting regularly to provide updates on transport implementation
Establish excellent relationship with transport vendor(s) at the market level
Intake all circuit design and expedite ordering and testing related activities with transport engineers and provisioners
Post Deployment issue resolution, planning and analyzing to ensure proper coverage when traffic drastically increases due to external factors
Troubleshoot and optimize traffic performance on live network
Plan and coordinate transport requirements for Special Events, DAS, and small cell projects
Skills, Experience and Requirements:

The successful Transport Specialist will have:
2-4+ years in the Telecom industry
Basic understanding of wireless data and voice network architecture
Advanced understanding of transport implementation
Experience with Microsoft Office and Google Suite
Ability to identify and understand complex problems/issues and develop solutions
Ability to manage multiple commitments simultaneously
Excellent written and oral communication skills
Excellent attention to details
Outstanding organizational and analytical skills
Able to work independently and inside a team
Bachelor' Degree or an equivalent combination of education and experience.
