WHAT YOU'LL DO
The Compliance Team promotes the development of a positive compliance culture, ethical decision making throughout BCG via the effective management of compliance programs. The team develops the strategy, capabilities, knowledge, tools and trainings to empower BCG staff to act in accordance with our Values and Code of Conduct.
In this position you will be working closely with the Compliance team assisting us to design and implement various compliance projects (e.g., develop and execute compliance trainings, draft ‘how-to’ compliance toolkits and guidance, provide education on policies and ethical issues, produce critical insights on internal and external compliance trends and topics, develop compliance dashboards and board level reporting to inform decision making,) at BCG.
You will learn about compliance risks and regulatory compliance topics that BCG as a global organization faces on a day-to-day basis as well as how our team identifies and prioritizes those compliance risks. Finally, you will learn about what actions we take to mitigate compliance risk through different compliance initiatives and how we manage cultural change.
Additionally, you will participate in cross-functional project collaboration and teaming in a complex global corporation while working with a team of international colleagues and internal customers.
