Data Analytics Lab
Primary functions
The Data Analytics Lab is looking for a dedicated and detail-oriented professional to help support a new research initiative assessing the attitudes of Africans on several topics. This role will aid in the creation of digitized data that can be used to support the mission of ACSTRAP. We are looking for fellows, research associates, as well as interns. The ideal candidate will be able to effectively meet deadlines and possesses the ability to work remotely. S/he is skilled at detail, communication, data analysis, and is comfortable conducting desk research as needed. Candidates must be able to dedicate at least 5-10 hours a week to the initiative. Candidates must be team-oriented and passionate about African issues. Education in a qualitative and quantitative methods, international relations, policy, international studies, African studies, or any other related discipline is preferred.
