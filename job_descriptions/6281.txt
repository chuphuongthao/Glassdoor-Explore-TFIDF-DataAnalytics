About the role:
Insurity's Next Solution Consultant of Data & Analytics
The Solution Consulting team at Insurity is responsible for improving, performing, and maintaining the active sales lifecycle for all Insurity products. They interact with prospective customers through discovery conversations, solution demonstrations, presentations, and follow-up discussions.
This role will blend art & science – the art of expanding the Insurity philosophy of making insurance technology solutions simple, combined with the science of understanding how to build trust in future customer partnerships through product demonstrations and industry knowledge.
Reporting to the VP of Solution Consulting, the Solution Consultant is responsible for working with and assisting sales executives in the discovery process to determine the high-level and detailed requirements of the prospect and the supporting business case. The goal of the solution consultant during an active sales cycle is to gain acceptance from the customer that Insurity can solve the customer’s problem and is the right choice over the other competitive offerings.
During the sales cycle, they support all phases and are directly responsible for product demonstrations, product knowledge, and contribution to RFP/RFI responses.
What you’ll be doing:
In advance of a demonstration or presentation, conduct discovery sessions with sales executives and representatives from the prospective customer in order to understand their needs and gain agreement on business requirements and steps in the evaluation process
Compose and deliver value-based software demonstrations/presentations in-person and remotely covering Insurity solutions to prospective customer audiences in support of sales cycles. Preparation includes personalization of materials to articulate sales messages and differentiating Insurity from other options in the market
Support sales executives by leading the coordination and completion of customer proposals, RFPs / RFIs, written responses, and other required documentation
Provide limited post-sale support to key customers primarily to the project/implementation team to ensure a smooth transition
Create and deliver presentations at industry events and trade shows
Work with the Market Solutions team to maintain a strong database of competitive information
What you’ll need:
BA/BS degree required or adequate industry experience
Minimum of 5-7 years of applicable sales/sales engineer experience in a solution-based complex environment with a high familiarity in the full sales process
Commercial insurance industry knowledge
Experience with Database architecture, analytics, machine learning, and/or artificial intelligence
Proven success providing presentations to non-technical audiences and articulating business value
Demonstrate outstanding verbal, written communication and presentation skills with the ability to tailor communications to the appropriate audience
Expert knowledge in end-to-end processes and solution matching
Highly organized with strong planning skills, delivering on-time and to high-quality standards, able to prioritize multiple projects simultaneously
Ability to travel up to 50%
