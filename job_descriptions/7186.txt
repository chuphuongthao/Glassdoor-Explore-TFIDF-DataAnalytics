Job Description
Overview
Vertex is seeking a Contractor with expertise in data analytics for a long-term assignment with the Marketing Science team. This group is responsible for delivering integrated insights that drive critical business decisions through 'voice of the customer' and data analytics. This is an exciting opportunity to be a part of a cohesive team that is supporting Vertex’s efforts to deliver transformative medicines in Cystic Fibrosis and other serious diseases.
Position Summary
This person will be responsible for delivering commercial insights from data analytics to inform strategic choices and commercialization decisions across multiple disease areas in the Vertex portfolio. They will collaborate with colleagues in Marketing Science, as well as a cross-functional team, to maintain a pulse on critical business questions, formulate and execute an analytics plan to answer them.
Key Duties & Responsibilities
Leverage Vertex’s in-licensed real world data assets to conduct queries and analyses to support the entire product portfolio and pipeline disease areas
Support the Marketing Science disease area leads to assess knowledge gaps and objectives that may be addressed by data analytics
Formulate and execute a comprehensive analytics generation plan to address a spectrum of business questions using the appropriate data source & methodology
Participate in multiple disease area analytics coordination forums, which provide cross-functional alignment on analytics activities supporting that program
Visualize and communicate the output of analyses, including both written and verbal communication, to business leaders and non-technical audiences
Work independently to set priorities and develop deliverables on projects
Required Education Level
Bachelor's (and often graduate) degree in a quantitative, analytical discipline, such as Operations Research, Applied Mathematics, Management Science, Data Science, Statistics, Econometrics, or Engineering.
Required Experience
Typically requires 10 or more years in bio-pharma analytics and/or market research, consulting or the equivalent combination of education and experience
Ability to manage offshore analytics personnel
Required Knowledge & Skills
Domain knowledge in clinical data, real world data (e.g. DRG, TriNetX, IBM MarketScan, IQVIA Pharmetrics, Premier or others), and/or other related data sources such as providers and health systems
Expertise with programming languages such as SAS, Python or others, and database languages such as SQL
Strong complex quantitative analysis skills; experience analyzing patient-level data within health databases, as well as partnering with advanced analytics professionals, and interpreting results
Experience with understanding and leveraging machine learning output
Exceptional written and verbal communication skills – able to synthesize and distill complex information into a compelling narrative
Ability to work and communicate with non-technical teams to translate business questions to analytically actionable questions
Proactive orientation - able to anticipate issues, engage with stakeholders and plan accordingly
Demonstrated experience as a trusted advisor and thought partner to stakeholders
Comfortable with ambiguity; able to effectively structure problems and solution approaches
