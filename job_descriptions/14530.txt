What is Underdog?

Founded by a team of industry veterans, Underdog was born with the idea that bringing strategic innovation to sports gaming and entertainment is the key to success in an exceptionally competitive market. Underdog has quickly established itself as a leader in the space, and is committed to building the best sports gaming platform in the industry - and supporting the most passionate community of sports fans while doing it!

At Underdog, we're not only about creating these awesome products, but also about growing our culture of passion, ownership, and fun! We believe that great companies are made out of great people. Our continual aim is to create an inclusive environment for everyone, at all levels, to achieve their highest potential at work.

As the Director of Data, you will be responsible for crafting and developing a holistic vision of the data organization to support the strategic goals of Underdog. Reporting directly to the CTO, you will lead the Data Engineering, Business Intelligence, and Data Science teams in the execution of the overall vision, and serve as a mentor and collaborator for your team in defining, scoping, and solving problems with data.
Please note, Underdog is a US based company and no sponsorship is available for this position at this time.

What you'll do:
Create a broad vision for the future of the data organization at Underdog; identify the ways we can use our data to know our customers better, improve internal processes, create efficiencies and competitive advantages, and positively impact the bottom line; report to the CTO and work with the executive team to continuously improve, recalibrate, and refine the vision in keeping with Underdog's evolving needs
Develop strategic plans to accomplish the vision; work with the Product Manager to create a roadmap that clearly defines ownership and responsibility between the related teams, and develop KPIs to track progress
Work with the BI team to develop a robust self-service offering to allow stakeholders across the company to interact with data and take action accordingly
Lead qualitative and quantitative analysis of industry trends and the competitive landscape to highlight improvements and growth opportunities, identifying key business challenges and developing insights and recommendations to address them
Build a Data Science & Insights team (which includes predictive analytics) within the data department to facilitate deeper analysis and hypothesis testing with regard to business questions; create an environment of collaboration between this and the BI team to deliver those insights to senior leadership
Grow, manage, and support the Business Intelligence & Analytics, Data Engineering, and Data Science teams, ensuring the work being performed is in line with the broader objectives of the department
Identify and lead the evaluation of new technologies that could potentially solve problems in the data department and other departments, engaging key stakeholders from impacted teams for input
Own and manage the data department's budget
Establish career paths for the various roles in the department and take an active role in developing the talent of the team members and supporting them in their career goals
Work with the Data Engineering team to ensure the company's data is being ingested, stored, and accessed in accordance with best practices, and in a tool-agnostic way that properly facilitates analysis (i.e., the data structure isn't dependent on any BI or analysis tool)
Who you are:
A transformational leader with a passion for data and analysis, who believes in the power of data to drive and calibrate a company's strategic objectives and has experience crafting a vision for how to do so from the ground up
Minimum of 5 years experience in a senior leadership position managing a BI and/or analytics team
Minimum of 10 years experience working with data in a BI, data science, data engineering, or analytics capacity
Bachelor's degree in business, computer science, management information systems, or a related field
An extremely effective communicator, both verbally and in writing, who values relationships and constant, clear communication
An effective manager who is comfortable giving regular feedback and coaching
Curious, full of questions, and passionate about solving problems
Experienced in leveraging organizational data for competitive advantage as well as for internal analysis and improvement
Experienced in a high-growth company, where the landscape and corresponding needs evolve and change quickly
Experienced with SQL and relational databases
Experienced with a cloud-based BI tool such as Tableau
Experienced with predictive analytics and/or machine learning
Able to work cross-functionally to investigate problems and gather the necessary resources to solve them
Even better if you have…
An advanced degree in a quantitative or computer science field
Hands-on experience with Google Cloud Platform
Exposure to Radical Candor
