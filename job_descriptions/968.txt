As a Business Intelligence Engineer in the Wegmans IT Department, you will be responsible for driving business intelligence (BI) initiatives and solutions, serving as the subject matter expert for Wegmans data and systems environment by understanding our data, how it is used by the business, and the relationship between systems. You will assist both the IT Developmental and Technical Services teams in architecting and standardizing the BI strategy, tools and BI meta-data life cycle, to be used across the organization.
What will I do?
Collaborate with product, technical, and operational leaders to gather data and metrics requirements
Produce comprehensive, usable dataset documentation, metadata, BI reports, dashboards and insights; Expand self-service capabilities with interactive dashboards, reports, and other visualizations
Partner with other BI team members to design, develop, and support semantic layers
Create, maintain and champion the use of processes and tools to promote and facilitate mapping of data to BI sources and interfaces
Develop BI data management methodology and best practices and educate business users who create their own content
Collaborate and facilitate with business data analysts and stakeholders to align outcomes to data requirements
Provide consulting services to analyst, data science, and software development teams
Establish measurements and processes to chart and assess progress related to completeness and quality of BI information
Required Qualifications:
Bachelor’s Degree in a related field
Experience with Business Intelligence Development and in shaping enterprise BI solutions, including architecting and BI data modeling
Project coordination experience
Database proficiency (ex. Oracle, Microsoft SQL, Netezza)
Presentation and technical problem-solving skills
Preferred Qualifications:
Master’s Degree in a related field
