Dematic is looking for a high caliber Data Analyst who will work with a global team of other data analysts as well as business process owners.

Dematic is developing and enhancing its global data analyst skillset to support the company with advanced data analysis, analytics, trending, statistical forecasting and demand planning, identification of leading indicators, and other beneficial uses of data for improved decision making and tracking of important KPIs and metrics.
What we offer:
This Is What You Will Do In This Role:
Interpret data, analyze results using statistical techniques and provide ongoing reports and analysis
Work independently and within a team to develop and implement databases, data collection systems, data analytics and other strategies that optimize statistical efficiency and quality
Acquire data from primary or secondary data sources
Identify, analyze, and interpret trends or patterns in complex data sets
Identify data cleansing opportunities within our source systems, and suggest ways to improve data accuracy and/or collection of new data points
Work with management to prioritize business and information needs
Locate and define new process improvement opportunities
Expected benefits and focus of the role
Aid in the implementation and maintenance of a statistical forecast while understanding the legacy demand plan
Provide BI reports and support to teams across the globe
Be part of a team that provides daily updated reports via QMC and a Qlik access portal to a large internal customer base
 Provide process insights and improvements both to the customer base and within the Data team
Tasks and Qualifications:
This Is What We Are Looking For:
BS in Mathematics, Computer Science, Information Management, or Statistics or similar
Experience with BI tools such as QlikView or Power BI most useful
Knowledge of SQL, SAP, Excel and Snowflake
Strong analytical skills with the ability to collect, organize, analyze, and disseminate significant amounts of information with attention to detail and accuracy
Proven ability presenting data findings in simple and easy to understand ways
Technical expertise regarding data models, database design, data mining and segmentation techniques
Familiarity with ERP systems & underlying data sources, SAP most useful
An understanding of statistical forecasting and demand planning
Ability to work with different cultures
Demonstrated outstanding team interaction and communication skills
Proven history of successful problem-solving
Excellent MS office skills
Be comfortable working in a ticketing system and an Agile structure
