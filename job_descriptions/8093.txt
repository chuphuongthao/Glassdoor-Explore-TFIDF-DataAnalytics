Position PurposeThe Health Informatics Data Analyst implements and optimizes data analytics, reporting and data visualization tools to support the Clinical, Quality, Prevention and Social Services operations of Vivent Health. As such, the Health Informatics Data Analyst understands the data landscape at Vivent Health and marries this knowledge with stakeholder and business requirements to provide accurate, insightful, and actionable information, and by doing so, directly supports the holistic patient-centric care model at Vivent Health.NOTE: This role could be located in Wisconsin (various locations), Denver, St. Louis or Austin! ******It is NOT a remote position******Scope of ResponsibilitiesVivent Health is one of the nation’s largest providers of HIV prevention, care and treatment services with a budget approaching $225 million and employing over 450 professionals.The unique, nationally recognized Vivent Health HIV Medical home model of care assures that everyone with HIV has access to medical, dental, mental health and social services and provides the best opportunity for patients to achieve high quality health outcomes. Vivent Health also provides aggressive HIV prevention services to gay men, injection drug users and others at the highest risk for HIV infection.Vivent Health currently operates in multiple states nationwide. It embraces a Market Culture to increase access to care, enhance quality outcomes, achieve, and surpass financial performance expectations and has an aggressive plan to expand into additional states.Essential FunctionsAt a high level, the Health Informatics Data Analyst transforms data into knowledge. To do this requires organizing and managing large and varied datasets from disparate sources, analyzing these datasets to optimize operations, and communicating findings using data visualization and reporting techniques to stakeholders and organizational leadership.
